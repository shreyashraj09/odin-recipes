Veg Pulao

About Pulao

Pulau is a one pot rice made by cooking fragment basmati rice with aromatic whole spices,herbs & sometimes stock. There are many kinds of pulau made in india like vegetable pulao, matar pulao, tawa pulao, coconut milk pulao

This Veg Pulao is the most basic dish & is made often in many indian homes. You will also find it always on the party buffet & celebration menus, served along with a yogurt raita & sometimes a kurma/korma/shorba.

ingredients & substitutes

  1. Spices
  2. Ginger garlic paste
  3. Herbs
  4. Rice
  5. Mixed Vegetables

Preparartion for pulao

you need to prepare the ingredients first. Rinse 11/2 cups basmati ice a few times until the water runs clear. Then soak it and set aside or at least 15 to 20 mins. Meanwhile prepare the veggies.

  • 1 medium onion thinly sliced & 1 green chilli slit
  • 1 medium carrot (chopped)
  • half cup green
  • 4 french beans
  • Handful of mint leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste

Tempering

  1. Add 2 tablespoons oil or ghee to a cooker or pot. add whole spices -
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 2 inch cinnamon
    • 4 cloves
    • 4 green cardamom
    • 1 teaspoon shahi jeera
    • 1 star anise
    • 1 pinch nutmeg
    • little dagad phool
    • 1 small strand mace

    Steps

  2. When the spices begin to sizzle, add one thinly sliced onions. And then 1 to 2 slit green chilies. saute them well
  3. When the onions turn golden,add 1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste
  4. Saute well until the raw smell of ginger garlic disappers. Do not burn
  5. Add mixed veggies and chopped mint/ pudina. I use peas,beans and carrots most of the times . You can also use Potatoes and a handful of of split cashews or softened soya chunk if you like
  6. Add mixed veggies and chopped mint / pudina . I use peas, beans and carots most of the times. You can also use potatoes and handful of split cashews or softened soya chunks if you like
  7. Next saute everything well until the mint smells good. Usually it takes About2 to 3 minutes
  8. Pour 2 cups water if making in cooker and then add 1 teaspoon salt as well
  9. Bring the water to a rolling boil, then add soaked & drained rice. Ensure there is no water in the rice, otherwise the veg pulao will turn mushy
  10. Stir gently and taste test the water. If needed add more salt
